Audience

The main audince will be people in the United States planning a trip, using their own vehicles as transportation. The age range is 17-28 and 45-60 (basically adults that don't have kids living at home.)


Josie: 20 year old woman planning an inpromtu road trip with friends.


Scenarios:
  1. Will there be any major weather concerns on the road from A to B?
  2. How does the temperature and humidity compare to my hometown? (can I compare the two on the website?)
  3. Is there a pressure difference between the places that will affect my tires?
  4. What will the forecast be for the week I am there?
  5. Does it rain a lot in town?

Paul:50 year old man planning a trip for his wedding aniversary.


Scenarios:
  1. What is the average temperature of place I'm trying to visit at a specific time of year?
  2. Is it likely to rain at this time of year?
  3. How does the humidity compare to my hometown?
  4. Is there a large pressure or elevation difference that could cause some discomfort the first few days there?
